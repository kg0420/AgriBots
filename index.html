About Our Project

AgriBotics is an innovative solution that integrates IoT, automation, and AI to revolutionize farming. Our system enhances precision agriculture by optimizing resource usage and improving yield predictions.

Key Features:

  • Real-time Monitoring: Soil health, moisture, temperature, and air quality tracking.
  • Automated Irrigation: Smart water management based on soil moisture levels.
  • AI-Powered Pest Detection: Early pest identification using AI models.
  • Weather Forecasting: Predicting weather conditions for optimized farming strategies.
  • Mobile App Integration: Live data and alerts accessible via a mobile dashboard.
  • Solar-Powered System: Sustainable energy solution for uninterrupted operations.

Our Vision:

To empower farmers with cutting-edge technology that enhances productivity, reduces waste, and promotes sustainable agriculture.

Our Team:

Led by Krish Gupta and his dedicated team, we bring expertise in IoT, AI, and agriculture to create a seamless, automated, and data-driven farming experience.
